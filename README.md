#  AI_PD – Artificial Intelligence Product Development

## 📌 Overview

This repository contains assignments and project work for the **AI_PD (Artificial Intelligence Product Development)** course. It focuses on designing, developing, and deploying AI-based solutions for real-world problems across various industries.

---

##  Objectives

* Apply AI techniques to solve real-world problems
* Develop and evaluate AI models using modern frameworks
* Understand AI deployment strategies (Cloud, Edge, On-Premise)
* Explore business, ethical, and security aspects of AI systems

---

##  Contents

* AI-based problem identification & solution design
* Model development using TensorFlow / PyTorch
* Sentiment analysis on customer and social media data
* AI deployment strategy comparison (Cloud vs Edge)
* Business Model Canvas for AI products
* Ethical analysis of AI applications
* AI model building, testing, and evaluation
* AI system architecture design
* AI security and adversarial protection techniques

---

##  Technologies Used

* Python
* TensorFlow / PyTorch
* Scikit-learn
* NLP (NLTK / Transformers)
* Pandas, NumPy

---

##  Learning Outcomes

* Develop AI-based solutions for real-world applications
* Build scalable and efficient AI models
* Analyze AI product case studies
* Evaluate business feasibility and impact of AI systems
